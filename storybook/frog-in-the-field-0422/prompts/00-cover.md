# Cover

Children's picture book illustration, soft watercolor, warm colors, round shapes, simple background, for 0-5 years old.

A wide golden rice field under a bright blue sky with fluffy white clouds and gentle rolling hills in the distance. A chubby little green frog (Guagua) with big round golden eyes, white belly, wearing bright yellow rain boots and a small lily pad hat, stands on a large green lily pad with arms wide open, welcoming the sunshine. A small pink snail (Wowo) with a brown and cream spiral shell, wearing a blue scarf, slowly crawls on the muddy ground near the lily pad. The field has golden rice stalks, colorful wildflowers, and butterflies. The overall scene is warm, bright, and full of life.

Title text in Chinese: "田野里的青蛙"

Consistent character design, 16:9 aspect ratio.
