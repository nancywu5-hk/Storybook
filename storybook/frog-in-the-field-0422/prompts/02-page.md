# Page 2

Children's picture book illustration, soft watercolor, warm colors, round shapes, simple background, for 0-5 years old.

A small pink snail (Wowo) with a brown and cream spiral-patterned shell, wearing a blue scarf around its neck, slowly crawling along a little path at the edge of a rice field. In the background, the chubby green frog (Guagua) with yellow rain boots and lily pad hat is hopping toward Wowo from a distance, waving happily. Soft morning light, peaceful countryside scene.

Consistent character design, 16:9 aspect ratio.
