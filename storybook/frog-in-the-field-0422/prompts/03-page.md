# Page 3

Children's picture book illustration, soft watercolor, warm colors, round shapes, simple background, for 0-5 years old.

The chubby green frog (Guagua) with big golden eyes, yellow rain boots and lily pad hat is crouching excitedly in front of the small pink snail (Wowo) with brown-cream spiral shell and blue scarf. Guagua is gesturing enthusiastically with both little hands, eyes sparkling with excitement. Wowo is smiling and nodding gently. Behind them stretches a wide open countryside field, and far in the distance, a patch of white dandelion flowers can be seen. Bright sunny day, warm atmosphere.

Consistent character design, 16:9 aspect ratio.
