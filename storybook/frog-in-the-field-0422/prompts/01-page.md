# Page 1

Children's picture book illustration, soft watercolor, warm colors, round shapes, simple background, for 0-5 years old.

Early morning in a lush green rice paddy field, golden sunlight streaming across the green rice plants. A chubby little green frog (Guagua) with big round golden eyes, white belly, wearing bright yellow rain boots and a small lily pad hat, is joyfully hopping along a narrow dirt path between rice paddies, mouth wide open as if singing happily. Several colorful butterflies flutter around him, and the rice stalks sway gently in the breeze. The scene is cheerful and energetic.

Consistent character design, 16:9 aspect ratio.
