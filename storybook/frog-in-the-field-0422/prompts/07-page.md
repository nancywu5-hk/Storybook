# Page 7

Children's picture book illustration, soft watercolor, warm colors, round shapes, simple background, for 0-5 years old.

On a countryside path, the chubby green frog (Guagua) with yellow rain boots and lily pad hat has hopped back and found the small pink snail (Wowo) with brown-cream shell and blue scarf stopped by the roadside. Guagua tilts his head curiously, looking at Wowo. Wowo is leaning forward with a very focused, concentrated expression, looking intently at something small on the ground that we can't quite see yet. The scene is warm and curious, with green grass and wildflowers along the path.

Consistent character design, 16:9 aspect ratio.
