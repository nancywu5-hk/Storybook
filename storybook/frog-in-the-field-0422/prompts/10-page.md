# Page 10

Children's picture book illustration, soft watercolor, warm colors, round shapes, simple background, for 0-5 years old.

Beautiful warm golden sunset scene. The chubby green frog (Guagua) with yellow rain boots and lily pad hat and the small pink snail (Wowo) with brown-cream shell and blue scarf are together in a field of white dandelion puffs. Guagua puffs up his round cheeks and blows hard on a big dandelion, sending hundreds of tiny white fluffy seeds floating across the golden-orange sunset sky. Wowo tilts his head up watching the dandelion seeds dance in the air, smiling with pure joy. In the far background, a large gentle old water buffalo (Uncle Niu) with dark grey skin, curved horns, and grass-green apron watches them warmly with a kind smile. The entire scene is bathed in warm golden light, radiating happiness and friendship.

Consistent character design, 16:9 aspect ratio.
