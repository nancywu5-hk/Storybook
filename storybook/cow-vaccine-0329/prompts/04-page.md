# Page 4

Children's picture book illustration, soft watercolor, warm colors, round shapes, simple background, for 0-5 years old. 16:9 aspect ratio.

A small fluffy white lamb (Miemie) with an orange star-patterned scarf extends both small paws, each holding a beautifully wrapped candy — one red and one yellow. She smiles warmly and offers them to her friend. A small baby calf (Moumou) with light brown and white fur looks up, tears still on his face but curious about the candies. The background is a cozy garden by the clinic entrance with soft morning light.

Consistent character design.
