# Page 6

Children's picture book illustration, soft watercolor, warm colors, round shapes, simple background, for 0-5 years old. 16:9 aspect ratio.

A small fluffy white lamb (Miemie) with an orange scarf uses her soft little hoof to tightly hold the hoof of a small baby calf (Moumou). Her expression is gentle yet determined. The calf looks at the lamb, his expression changing from scared to hesitant, then he nods softly. They stand at the clinic entrance, hand in hoof, ready to go in together. The background shows the welcoming clinic doorway with warm light spilling out.

Consistent character design.
