# Page 1

Children's picture book illustration, soft watercolor, warm colors, round shapes, simple background, for 0-5 years old. 16:9 aspect ratio.

A sunny morning on a green meadow. A small baby calf (Moumou) with light brown and white patchy fur, wearing sky blue overalls and a small red baseball cap with a yellow bell, walks hand-in-hoof with a small fluffy white lamb (Miemie) wearing an orange scarf with white stars. They walk along a path holding hands. In the distance, a forest clinic with a red roof is visible. Birds fly in the sky, wildflowers bloom by the roadside. The calf's tail sways gently. Both look cheerful and relaxed.
