Character reference sheet for a children's picture book. White background. Show each character from front view and side view. Soft watercolor style, warm colors, round shapes, cute and friendly. For 0-5 year old readers.

Character 1 - Moumou (baby calf): Light brown and white patchy short fur, like milk chocolate with cream. A white heart-shaped marking on the center of forehead. Big round dark brown eyes with long eyelashes. Pink moist little nose. Wearing sky blue overalls with rolled-up cuffs showing pink hooves. A small red baseball cap with a tiny yellow bell on head. Round and chubby toddler proportions (about 3 years old). Innocent and slightly timid expression.

Character 2 - Miemie (baby lamb): Fluffy snow-white curly wool all over body, like cotton candy. Light pink face with floppy little ears. Gentle light blue eyes, always smiling. An orange scarf with white little stars around the neck. Slimmer and softer appearance than the calf. Warm and gentle expression.

Character 3 - Dr. Giraffe (giraffe doctor): Light yellow fur with dark brown net-like patterns. Long elegant neck curved gently. Round gold-rimmed glasses with a chain dangling by the ear. White doctor's coat with a stethoscope around the neck. A rainbow-colored pen in the chest pocket. Doctor's cap on head. Tall but moves gently and softly. Kind and patient expression.

Consistent character design. Children's picture book illustration style.
