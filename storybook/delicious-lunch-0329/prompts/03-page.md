# Page 3

Children's picture book illustration, soft watercolor, warm colors, round shapes, simple background, for 0-5 years old. 16:9 aspect ratio.

The pink chubby baby pig (Xiao Zhu) leans close to the puppy's plate, his pink nose twitching, eyes fixed on the fragrant hot dog. The golden yellow puppy (Xiao Gou) is about to eat his lunch but stops when he sees the pig's expression. The pig's plate has only a small bite taken from the sandwich.

Consistent character design.
