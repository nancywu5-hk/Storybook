# Page 9

Children's picture book illustration, soft watercolor, warm colors, round shapes, simple background, for 0-5 years old. 16:9 aspect ratio.

The pink chubby baby pig (Xiao Zhu) and the golden yellow puppy (Xiao Gou) happily share their lunch, passing food to each other. The pig passes apple slices to the puppy, the puppy passes broccoli to the pig. Both have joyful smiles on their faces, their tails (and curly tail) wagging happily. The dining room is filled with laughter and cheer. A lively, happy sharing moment.

Consistent character design.
