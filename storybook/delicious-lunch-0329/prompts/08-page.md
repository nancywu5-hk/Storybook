# Page 8

Children's picture book illustration, soft watercolor, warm colors, round shapes, simple background, for 0-5 years old. 16:9 aspect ratio.

The pink chubby baby pig's (Xiao Zhu) eyes glisten with tears, but the corners of his mouth turn up. He uses his small trotters to place all the carrot sticks from his plate onto the center of the table, pushing them toward the puppy. The golden yellow puppy (Xiao Gou) happily accepts the carrots. The two lunches become one big shared plate in the middle. A touching moment of sharing.
