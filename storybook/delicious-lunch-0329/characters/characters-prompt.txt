Character reference sheet for a children's picture book. White background. Show each character from front view and side view. Soft watercolor style, warm colors, round shapes, cute and friendly. For 0-5 year old readers.

Character 1 - Xiao Zhu (baby pig): Pink chubby round body, short curly tail like a question mark. Big floppy ears with slightly upturned tips. Round black eyes, pink pig nose that sniffs around. Wearing a yellow striped T-shirt, round belly showing. Brown little boots on feet. Waddling toddler proportions (about 3 years old). Cute and food-loving expression.

Character 2 - Xiao Gou (puppy): Golden yellow and white short fur, floppy ears with white ear tips. Round shiny black eyes, wet black nose. Tongue often sticking out. Wearing blue overalls with a small bone pattern on the chest. Fluffy tail that wags when happy. Slimmer and more agile than the pig.

Character 3 - Teacher Goat (goat teacher): Snow white curly fur, a goatee on the chin. Curved horns with a blue ribbon tied around them. Gold-rimmed glasses on face, looking scholarly. Wearing a light purple dress with a white apron. Holding a storybook or recipe book. Slim and elegant posture, gentle demeanor.

Consistent character design. Children's picture book illustration style.
