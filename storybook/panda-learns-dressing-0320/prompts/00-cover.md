# Cover

Children's picture book cover illustration, soft watercolor style, warm bright colors, round shapes, simple composition, for 0-5 years old.

A chubby giant panda toddler (black-and-white, round black ears, dark eye patches, wearing light blue overalls over white T-shirt) stands in the center, one hand holding up a red jacket, the other arm stuck in the wrong sleeve hole, with a determined yet comically confused expression. A small brown monkey toddler (peach face, yellow striped T-shirt, green shorts, curly tail) stands beside him, covering his mouth giggling.

Background: a bright and cheerful kindergarten cloakroom with colorful little jackets hanging on low hooks.

Title text in playful rounded Chinese font at the top: "熊猫宝贝学穿衣"

Consistent character design, warm lighting, joyful mood.
