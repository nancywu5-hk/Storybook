# Page 9

Children's picture book illustration, soft watercolor style, warm bright colors, round shapes, simple background, for 0-5 years old.

A small brown monkey toddler (peach face, yellow jacket over yellow striped T-shirt, green shorts, curly tail) spreads open a red jacket in front of a chubby giant panda toddler (black-and-white, round ears, dark eye patches). The monkey points with one little finger at the label inside the collar of the jacket, like a little teacher showing the way. The panda watches carefully and is stretching one arm toward the sleeve opening, learning.

Bright warm lighting, encouraging and hopeful mood, consistent character design.
