# 第11页 - 美好的回忆

Children's book illustration in soft watercolor style. Sunset scene on a meadow. The golden puppy and white kitten sit side by side watching the beautiful orange and pink sunset. Next to them is a basket of mushrooms they collected. The puppy has his head on the kitten's shoulder, both look content and happy. Warm, nostalgic atmosphere. Cute Axel Scheffler inspired style.
