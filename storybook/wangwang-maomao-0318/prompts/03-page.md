# 第3页 - 成为朋友

Children's book illustration in soft watercolor style. The golden puppy (WangWang) is bouncing ahead happily on a forest path, tail wagging. The white kitten (MiaoMiao) walks gracefully behind, a gentle smile on her face. Surrounded by tall trees, dappled sunlight, mushrooms, and forest flowers. Best friends walking together on an adventure. Cute Axel Scheffler inspired style.
