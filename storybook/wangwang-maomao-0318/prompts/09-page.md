# 第9页 - 照顾小鸟

Children's book illustration in soft watercolor style. Under a big tree, the golden puppy and white kitten are tenderly caring for a small bird. The kitten gently offers water, the puppy watches with care. The bird looks better, starting to perk up. Warm, compassionate scene. Sunlight filters through tree branches. Cute Axel Scheffler inspired style.
