# 第6页 - 互相帮助

Children's book illustration in soft watercolor style. The brave golden puppy is swimming across the stream with the white kitten on his back. The kitten holds onto the puppy's fur gently, looking a bit nervous but trusting her friend. Water splashes around them. The puppy paddles determinedly. Heartwarming scene of friendship and teamwork. Cute Axel Scheffler inspired style.
