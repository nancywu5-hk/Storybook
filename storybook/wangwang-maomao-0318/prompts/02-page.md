# 第2页 - 不打不相识

Children's book illustration in soft watercolor style. The snowy white kitten with sapphire blue eyes is gently patting her fur clean, looking at the golden puppy with a slight smile. The puppy looks relieved and happy, tail starting to wag. Both are on a sunny meadow with colorful flowers around. Warm, friendly atmosphere. Cute Axel Scheffler inspired character design.
