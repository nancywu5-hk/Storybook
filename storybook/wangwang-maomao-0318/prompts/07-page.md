# 第7页 - 找到蘑菇

Children's book illustration in soft watercolor style. On the other side of the stream, the golden puppy and white kitten have found a wonderful patch of colorful mushrooms. They are happily picking mushrooms - the puppy using his mouth, the kitten using her paws. Big smiles on both faces. Beautiful forest background with dappled sunlight. Cute Axel Scheffler inspired style.
