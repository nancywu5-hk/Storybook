# 第 8 页 Prompt — 08-page

Children's picture book illustration, warm soft watercolor style, similar to Axel Scheffler. Consistent character design throughout. MOOD SHIFT BACK TO WARM - hope returns!

Underwater, inside the dark waterweed tangle, but now with a burst of warm light. PAOPAO, the round dark gray tadpole with long eyelashes, light blue tail tip, squeezes through a gap in the dense waterweed. The tiny yellow water lily flower on her head GLOWS like a small warm lantern, radiating soft golden-yellow light that pushes back the surrounding darkness. She swims right up to DOUDOU, the black tadpole with red scarf, and gently nuzzles her body against his, her expression tender and determined. Doudou lifts his head from his curled-up position, his wet big eyes now catching the warm light, his expression transforming from fear to surprise and relief. The two tiny tadpoles press close together. Warm golden light radiates outward from Paopao's little yellow flower, creating a glowing circle of warmth in the darkness. The contrast between the dark waterweed background and the warm central glow is dramatic and heartwarming. Soft watercolor, warm golden light against cool dark surroundings, emotional turning point composition.
