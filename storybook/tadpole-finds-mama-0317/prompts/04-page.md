# 第 4 页 Prompt — 04-page

Children's picture book illustration, warm soft watercolor style, similar to Axel Scheffler. Consistent character design throughout.

Pond bottom scene. UNCLE YUANYUAN, a large reddish-brown crab, crouches next to a big rock. He has two big impressive claws, eight legs, and eyes perched on long stalks. One eye stalk tilts sideways as he thinks, giving him a comically thoughtful expression. He looks serious but is actually gentle. He points one big claw toward the distance, indicating a direction. DOUDOU, a round shiny black tadpole with red scarf and waterweed curl bangs, floats in front of him with big wide eyes full of hope. PAOPAO, the smaller dark gray tadpole with yellow flower and long eyelashes, hides slightly behind Doudou, looking a bit scared of the big claws. The sandy bottom has small seashells and pebbles scattered around. Soft light filters down from above. Warm watercolor style, cute character expressions, gentle humor.
