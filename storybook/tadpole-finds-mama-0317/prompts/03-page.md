# 第 3 页 Prompt — 03-page

Children's picture book illustration, warm soft watercolor style, similar to Axel Scheffler. Consistent character design throughout.

Underwater scene in the middle of the pond. DOUDOU, a round shiny black tadpole with big sparkling eyes, tiny waterweed curl on head, and small red scarf, bravely swims up to AUNTIE JINJIN, a beautiful orange-red goldfish with long flowing tail fins that spread out like a gauze skirt, sparkling scales that catch the light. Doudou looks up at her with big hopeful eyes full of expectation. Auntie Jinjin looks down at the tiny tadpole with a warm, amused smile, her mouth slightly open. She is much bigger than Doudou, creating a sweet size contrast. PAOPAO, the dark gray tadpole with yellow flower on head, watches quietly from a short distance behind Doudou. A few strands of green waterweed and soft light beams in the clear water background. Soft watercolor textures, warm bright colors, cute and gentle mood.
