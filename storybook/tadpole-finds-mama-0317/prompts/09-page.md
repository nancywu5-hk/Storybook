# 第 9 页 Prompt — 09-page

Children's picture book illustration, warm soft watercolor style, similar to Axel Scheffler. Consistent character design throughout. JOYFUL DISCOVERY - bright and expansive!

A sudden burst of brightness and space! DOUDOU, the round shiny black tadpole with red scarf and waterweed bangs, and PAOPAO, the smaller dark gray tadpole with yellow flower on head, swim out from the edge of dark waterweed (visible at the left edge of the image) into a magnificent, sun-drenched lotus pond. The scene opens up dramatically - wide, bright, and beautiful. In the CENTER of the image, on the biggest, roundest, most vibrant green lily pad, sits MAMA QINGQING: a bright emerald green frog with a white round belly, big warm golden eyes looking down tenderly at the water, a gentle smile on her face, and a few dark green spots on her back. She sits elegantly on the lily pad. Pink lotus flowers bloom beside the lily pad. Doudou is swimming toward mama as fast as he can, his mouth wide open shouting with joy. Paopao swims beside him, happily swishing her tail. Brilliant golden sunlight floods the entire scene, the water surface glitters with golden sparkles. Everything is bright, warm, and bursting with joy. Soft watercolor, vivid warm bright colors, expansive joyful composition.
