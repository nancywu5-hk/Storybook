Children's picture book illustration, watercolor style, similar to Axel Scheffler. Consistent character design throughout. EMOTIONAL LOW POINT - somber intimate mood.

Close-up shot, underwater. DOUDOU, the round shiny black tadpole with red scarf drooping limply, and waterweed curl bangs, is curled up tightly under a bent piece of waterweed. His round body is scrunched into an even smaller ball, his tail curled inward. His big eyes are glistening wet, with a tiny crystal-clear droplet at the corner of his eye (indistinguishable from a water bubble or a tear). He looks utterly alone, scared, and sad. The color palette is cool blue and muted green tones, with dim lighting. Only the swaying dark waterweed and shadowy water surround him. The background is blurred and dark, focusing all attention on the small, vulnerable, lonely tadpole. Soft watercolor with cooler muted palette, intimate emotional close-up composition, conveying vulnerability and sadness.
