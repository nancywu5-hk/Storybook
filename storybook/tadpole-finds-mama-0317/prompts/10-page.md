# 第 10 页 Prompt — 10-page

Children's picture book illustration, warm soft watercolor style, similar to Axel Scheffler. Consistent character design throughout. WARM ENDING - the most tender, heartwarming scene.

On top of a large, beautiful green lily pad in golden sunset light. MAMA QINGQING, a bright emerald green frog with white belly, big warm golden eyes looking down lovingly, and dark green spots on her back, gently cups DOUDOU in both her green front feet (palms up, holding him tenderly). Doudou, the round shiny black tadpole with his red scarf fluttering gently in a light breeze, has his eyes closed in pure bliss, nestled in mama's palms with the most content, peaceful, happy expression. Mama lowers her head close to him, her golden eyes soft as sunlight, full of love. PAOPAO, the dark gray tadpole with long eyelashes and yellow water lily flower on her head (the flower bobbing gently), rests at the edge of the lily pad, watching the scene with a sweet, happy smile. Beside the lily pad, pink lotus flowers and green lotus seed pods. In the background, the pond glows with golden and orange sunset light. A few delicate dragonflies dance in the air. The whole scene radiates warmth, love, happiness, and completeness. This is the most beautiful, emotionally fulfilling page. Soft watercolor with warm golden sunset palette, intimate tender composition, pure joy and love.
