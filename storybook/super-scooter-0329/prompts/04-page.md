# Page 4

Children's picture book illustration, soft watercolor, warm colors, round shapes, simple background, for 0-5 years old. 16:9 aspect ratio.

The bunny (Tiaotiao) rides the scooter alone in the park, going around and around. His expression changes from happy to confused, then to slightly downcast. The park feels empty with only falling leaves drifting in the wind. The scooter's colorful glow seems dimmer. The scene conveys loneliness despite the fun activity.

Consistent character design.
