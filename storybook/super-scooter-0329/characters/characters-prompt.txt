Character reference sheet for a children's picture book. White background. Show each character from front view and side view. Soft watercolor style, warm colors, round shapes, cute and friendly. For 0-5 year old readers.

Character 1 - Tiaotiao (bunny): Soft light grey fur, long ears with white tips, big round shiny black eyes. Pink little nose that twitches slightly. Wearing an orange hoodie with small bunny ear shapes on the hood. Blue sneakers with white soles on feet. Slim and agile toddler proportions (about 3 years old). Energetic and bouncy posture.

Character 2 - Manman (turtle): Dark green shell with light yellow hexagonal patterns, rounded shell edges. Olive green skin, tiny black bean eyes, mouth always curved in a gentle smile. Red scarf with yellow tassels around the neck. Short stubby limbs. Much shorter than the bunny, round and chubby body. Slow but steady posture.

Character 3 - Uncle Bear (brown bear): Thick brown fur, round big belly, small round ears. Black-rimmed glasses on face, wearing blue overalls with a wrench in the chest pocket. Big padded paws. Tall and burly but gentle appearance.

Consistent character design. Children's picture book illustration style.
