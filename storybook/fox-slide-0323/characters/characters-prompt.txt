Character reference sheet for a children's picture book. White background. Show each character from front view and side view. Soft watercolor style, warm colors, round shapes, cute and friendly. For 0-5 year old readers.

Character 1 - Little Flame (small fox): Orange-red fur with creamy white belly and cheeks. Fluffy big tail with white tip. Wearing blue overalls/suspender shorts and a small yellow baseball cap. Round black eyes, small black nose. Short and chubby toddler proportions (about 3 years old).

Character 2 - Bubble (small hedgehog): Warm brown body with soft dark brown spines tipped with creamy yellow (rounded, not sharp). Wearing a green striped T-shirt. Short little legs. Round beady eyes, pink little nose. Shorter than the fox, very round and pudgy body shape.

Character 3 - Auntie Goose (big white goose): Snow white feathers, orange flat beak and orange webbed feet. Wearing a purple scarf around the neck and small round glasses. Tall and elegant posture.

Consistent character design. Children's picture book illustration style.
