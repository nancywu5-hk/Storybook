# Page 8

Children's picture book illustration, soft watercolor, warm colors, round shapes, simple background, for 0-5 years old.

A small orange-red fox cub (Little Flame) wearing blue overalls and a yellow baseball cap holds the hand of a small brown hedgehog (Bubble) wearing a green striped T-shirt. They stand together at the bottom of the rainbow slide stairs. Little Flame turns back to smile warmly and reassuringly at Bubble, extending his paw. Bubble holds Little Flame's hand tightly, still a bit nervous but nodding bravely. In the background, a tall white goose (Auntie Goose) wearing a purple scarf and round glasses watches them with a gentle, encouraging smile.

Consistent character design.
