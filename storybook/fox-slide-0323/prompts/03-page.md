# Page 3

Children's picture book illustration, soft watercolor, warm colors, round shapes, simple background, for 0-5 years old.

A small orange-red fox cub (Little Flame) wearing blue overalls and a yellow baseball cap has already climbed to the top of a rainbow slide. He leans over the railing waving down excitedly, calling to his friend below. At the bottom of the slide stairs, a small brown hedgehog (Bubble) wearing a green striped T-shirt grips the hem of his shirt nervously, his face scrunched up, looking up at the tall stairs with an anxious expression.

Consistent character design.
