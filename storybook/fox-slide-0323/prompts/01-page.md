# Page 1

Children's picture book illustration, soft watercolor, warm colors, round shapes, simple background, for 0-5 years old.

A warm sunny day. A small orange-red fox cub (Little Flame) wearing blue overalls and a yellow baseball cap holds hands with a small brown hedgehog (Bubble) wearing a green striped T-shirt. They walk together on a path leading to a park. Green bushes and wildflowers line both sides. Little Flame bounces happily, while Bubble takes small steps with his short legs trying to keep up. Both look cheerful.

Consistent character design.
