# Page 4

Children's picture book illustration, soft watercolor, warm colors, round shapes, simple background, for 0-5 years old.

A small orange-red fox cub (Little Flame) wearing blue overalls slides down a rainbow slide with arms spread wide, eyes closed, laughing joyfully. His yellow baseball cap is flying off his head. Speed lines show the fast movement. To the side of the slide, a small brown hedgehog (Bubble) wearing a green striped T-shirt stands alone, head lowered, small paws clutching his shirt hem, looking sad and left out.

Consistent character design.
