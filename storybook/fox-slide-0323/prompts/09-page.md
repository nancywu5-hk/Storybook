# Page 9

Children's picture book illustration, soft watercolor, warm colors, round shapes, simple background, for 0-5 years old.

Side view of a rainbow slide's staircase. A small orange-red fox cub (Little Flame) wearing blue overalls climbs ahead, turning back to extend his paw and wait patiently. A small brown hedgehog (Bubble) wearing a green striped T-shirt climbs behind him step by step, face tense but determined and trying hard. Warm golden sunlight streams down from above, illuminating them. Small footprint marks on the steps. The scene feels hopeful and encouraging.

Consistent character design.
