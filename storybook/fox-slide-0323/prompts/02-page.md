# Page 2

Children's picture book illustration, soft watercolor, warm colors, round shapes, simple background, for 0-5 years old.

A small brown hedgehog (Bubble) wearing a green striped T-shirt stands beside him, looking up at the tall slide with a worried, slightly scared expression.
