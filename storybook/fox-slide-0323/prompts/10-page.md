# Page 10

Children's picture book illustration, soft watercolor, warm colors, round shapes, simple background, for 0-5 years old.

A joyful climactic scene. A small orange-red fox cub (Little Flame) wearing blue overalls hugs a small brown hedgehog (Bubble) wearing a green striped T-shirt as they slide down the rainbow slide together. Little Flame has his arm around Bubble, both have their mouths wide open laughing with pure joy. Bubble's eyes are squeezed shut but he is smiling big. The wind blows Little Flame's yellow cap and Bubble's spines. Sparkles and happy light effects surround them. At the bottom of the slide, a tall white goose (Auntie Goose) wearing a purple scarf and round glasses opens her wings wide ready to catch them, beaming with a warm smile. Bright, vibrant, celebratory atmosphere.

Consistent character design.
