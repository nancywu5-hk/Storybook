# Page 5

Children's picture book illustration, soft watercolor, warm colors, round shapes, simple background, for 0-5 years old.

A split-panel composition showing three small scenes of a small orange-red fox cub (Little Flame) wearing blue overalls sliding down a rainbow slide three times in different poses — sitting upright, lying on belly, and with arms spread wide — all laughing happily. In the corner, a small brown hedgehog (Bubble) wearing a green striped T-shirt sits alone on a park bench, hugging his knees, head down, looking gloomy and lonely.

Consistent character design.
