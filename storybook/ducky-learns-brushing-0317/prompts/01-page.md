# Page 1 — 日常场景建立

Children's picture book illustration, soft watercolor style, warm and bright colors, round shapes, simple background, suitable for 0-5 years old kids, consistent character design, similar to Axel Scheffler's "Pip and Posy" style.

A bright cozy kitchen with warm morning sunlight streaming through a window. A round chubby bright yellow duckling (DUDU) with three tiny tufts of fuzz on head, wearing a sky-blue bib with a white star, sits at a small table happily eating bread with a big bite, jam smeared on the corners of his orange-red bill. Next to him, a fluffy snow-white lamb (MIANMIAN) with curly wool, wearing a mint-green neckerchief, eats neatly with small bites, her bib perfectly clean. On the table: bread, jam jar, and two glasses of milk. Cheerful and warm atmosphere.
