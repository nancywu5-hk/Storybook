Character reference sheet for a children's picture book. White background. Show each character from front view and side view. Soft watercolor style, warm colors, round shapes, cute and friendly. For 0-5 year old readers.

Character 1 - Pipi (small monkey): Golden-brown short fur, peach-colored face and belly, round big ears. Large deep brown eyes, small slightly upturned nose. Wearing a red vest with a small yellow star on it. Long curly tail with a dark brown tip. Short and chubby toddler proportions (about 3 years old). Very energetic and lively expression.

Character 2 - Manman (small turtle): Light green skin with a round dark green shell on the back, the shell has pale yellow hexagonal patterns. Tiny black bead eyes, mouth naturally curves up slightly like a gentle smile. Wearing blue striped shorts. Short stubby limbs. Shorter than the monkey, very round and pudgy. Calm and peaceful expression.

Character 3 - Auntie Hippo (hippo): Round and plump light purple-grey body, big wide mouth, small ears. Wearing an orange floral apron and a pink flower tucked behind one ear. A pearl necklace around the neck. Very large but gentle and warm appearance.

Consistent character design. Children's picture book illustration style.
