# Page 1

Children's picture book illustration, soft watercolor, warm colors, round shapes, simple background, for 0-5 years old. 16:9 aspect ratio.

A small golden-brown monkey (Pipi) wearing a red vest with a yellow star stands on a branch of a big tree at the edge of a forest. One hand shades his eyes as he looks into the distance eagerly. His curly tail wraps around the branch. A small suitcase sits at his feet on the branch. The forest is alive with distant silhouettes of a squirrel, butterflies, and birds. Bright, hopeful morning light. The monkey looks excited and full of anticipation.

Consistent character design.
