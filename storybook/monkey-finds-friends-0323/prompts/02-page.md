# Page 2

Children's picture book illustration, soft watercolor, warm colors, round shapes, simple background, for 0-5 years old. 16:9 aspect ratio.

A small golden-brown monkey (Pipi) wearing a red vest with a yellow star has rushed up to a small brown squirrel. The monkey grabs handfuls of acorns and throws them up into the air enthusiastically. Acorns scatter everywhere like rain, some landing on the squirrel's neatly stacked pile. The squirrel has wide frightened eyes, hugging its pile protectively. The monkey is laughing, completely unaware of the chaos. Forest floor with scattered leaves and acorns.

Consistent character design.
