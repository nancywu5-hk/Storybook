# Page 4

Children's picture book illustration, soft watercolor, warm colors, round shapes, simple background, for 0-5 years old. 16:9 aspect ratio.

A small golden-brown monkey (Pipi) wearing a red vest with a yellow star leaps into a pond with arms and legs spread wide, creating a huge splash. A small yellow duckling on the water is drenched by the splash, holding up its wings to shield its face, looking shocked and upset. Concentric ripples spread across the water surface. The monkey is mid-splash, looking excited and oblivious. Pond reeds and lily pads in the background.

Consistent character design.
