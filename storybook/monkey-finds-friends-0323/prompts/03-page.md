# Page 3

Children's picture book illustration, soft watercolor, warm colors, round shapes, simple background, for 0-5 years old. 16:9 aspect ratio.

A small brown squirrel clutches its acorns tightly, running away into the distance with a small bundle on its back, leaving a trail of tiny footprints. A small golden-brown monkey (Pipi) wearing a red vest with a yellow star stands alone where the squirrel was, tilting his head and scratching the back of his head with one paw. His curly tail droops limply. His expression is confused and puzzled. Scattered acorns lie on the ground around him.

Consistent character design.
