# Page 1

Children's picture book illustration, soft watercolor, warm colors, round shapes, simple background, for 0-5 years old. 16:9 aspect ratio.

On a forest path with wildflowers and small mushrooms along the roadside. A golden yellow baby bear (Xiao Xiong) wearing sky blue overalls and a red neckerchief holds hands with a reddish-brown red panda baby (Xiao Xiong Mao) wearing a light yellow T-shirt as they walk together. In the distance, a red roof and chimney smoke from Grandma Bear's house can be seen. Bright sunny day with warm sunlight.

Consistent character design.
