# Page 3

Children's picture book illustration, soft watercolor, warm colors, round shapes, simple background, for 0-5 years old. 16:9 aspect ratio.

In a cozy kitchen. Grandma Bear places two cups of steaming milk from a tray onto the table. The golden yellow baby bear (Xiao Xiong) eagerly lifts his cup with both paws and takes a big gulp, leaving a milk mustache on his mouth. The reddish-brown red panda baby (Xiao Xiong Mao) sits beside him, holding his cup with both hands and drinking in small sips. Warm kitchen atmosphere with soft lighting.

Consistent character design.
