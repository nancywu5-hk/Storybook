Character reference sheet for a children's picture book. White background. Show each character from front view and side view. Soft watercolor style, warm colors, round shapes, cute and friendly. For 0-5 year old readers.

Character 1 - Xiao Xiong (baby bear): Golden yellow fluffy soft fur, warm like honey. Round big head with two round ears standing up, inner ear is light pink. Big black shiny eyes, wet little black nose. Wearing sky blue overalls with rolled-up cuffs showing brown paws. Red small neckerchief around neck. Chubby toddler proportions (about 2-3 years old), waddling posture.

Character 2 - Xiao Xiong Mao (red panda baby): Reddish-brown soft long fur, white face with dark brown "black eye circles" around eyes like sunglasses. Big triangular ears with white ear tips. Fluffy big tail with alternating light and dark rings. Wearing a light yellow T-shirt. Slimmer and more agile than the bear.

Character 3 - Grandma Bear (bear grandma): Light brown fur, white knitted hat on head with a small red pom-pom. Reading glasses on face with a chain around the neck. Floral apron with a big pocket. Chubby and kind appearance, always smiling.

Consistent character design. Children's picture book illustration style.
