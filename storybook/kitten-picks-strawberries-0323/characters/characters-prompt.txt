Character reference sheet for a children's picture book. White background. Show each character from front view and side view. Soft watercolor style, warm colors, round shapes, cute and friendly. For 0-5 year old readers.

Character 1 - Huahua (small calico kitten): White short fur with irregular orange-yellow and light brown patches. Big round amber eyes, pink little nose and mouth. Wearing a pink polka-dot dress (white dots on pink). A small woven straw hat on head with a tiny daisy flower tucked in. Short tail that sticks up cutely. Round and chubby toddler proportions (about 3 years old).

Character 2 - Yuanyuan (small rabbit): Soft light grey-blue fur, long ears with white tips. Round black eyes, white belly. Wearing yellow overalls/suspender shorts with a small star embroidered on the pocket. Slightly taller and slimmer than the kitten.

Character 3 - Grandpa Goat (old goat): Grey-white fur, soft white beard on chin, small curved horns. Wearing a dark green apron with a small garden trowel peeking out of the pocket. Round gold-rimmed glasses. Tall but slightly hunched posture, kind and gentle appearance.

Consistent character design. Children's picture book illustration style.
