# Page 3

Children's picture book illustration, soft watercolor, warm colors, round shapes, simple background, for 0-5 years old. 16:9 aspect ratio.

Inside a lush strawberry garden. A small light grey-blue rabbit (Yuanyuan) wearing yellow overalls crouches among the strawberry plants, carefully examining a ripe red strawberry and gently placing it into her bamboo basket. Beside her, a small white calico kitten (Huahua) with orange patches, wearing a pink polka-dot dress and straw hat, also crouches picking strawberries but has leaned close to a big strawberry to sniff it, eyes half-closed, mouth slightly open, mesmerized by the sweet fragrance. Green leaves and red berries everywhere.

Consistent character design.
