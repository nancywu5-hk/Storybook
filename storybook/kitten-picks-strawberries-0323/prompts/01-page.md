# Page 1

Children's picture book illustration, soft watercolor, warm colors, round shapes, simple background, for 0-5 years old. 16:9 aspect ratio.

A country path leading to a beautiful strawberry garden with a wooden sign reading "Strawberry Garden" on the gate. A small white calico kitten (Huahua) with orange-yellow and light brown patches, wearing a pink polka-dot dress and a straw hat, bounces happily along the path holding hands with a small light grey-blue rabbit (Yuanyuan) wearing yellow overalls. Yuanyuan carries two small bamboo baskets. In the distance, the strawberry garden is full of green leaves and red strawberries, bathed in warm spring sunshine.

Consistent character design.
