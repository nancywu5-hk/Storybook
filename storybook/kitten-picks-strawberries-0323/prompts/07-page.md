# Page 7

Children's picture book illustration, soft watercolor, warm colors, round shapes, simple background, for 0-5 years old. 16:9 aspect ratio.

A small white calico kitten (Huahua) with orange patches, wearing a pink polka-dot dress and straw hat, rubs her eyes with one paw. Two big teardrops hang on her face. Her ears droop down, her tail hangs low. She looks guilty and sorry, still holding the nearly empty bamboo basket in her other paw. Warm golden sunlight casts a long shadow behind her. The strawberry garden is softly blurred in the background. Emotional, touching scene.
