Character reference sheet for a children's picture book, soft watercolor illustration style, warm and bright colors, white background, clean layout.

Character 1 — Caicai (Butterfly, toddler):
Front view and side view. A chubby round little butterfly with a pair of large rainbow-gradient wings (pink to orange-yellow to light purple from inside to outside), white polka dot patterns on the wings. Big round black eyes, two curly antennae on the head, six short little legs. Wearing a small pale yellow sun hat with a tiny daisy pinned on it. Shy, sweet expression. Age appearance: 3-4 years old toddler proportions, round and cute body shape.

Character 2 — Wengweng (Bee, toddler):
Front view and side view. A round chubby little bee with classic yellow-and-black stripes, small transparent buzzing wings, big round eyes that squint into crescent moons when smiling, round rosy cheeks, short antennae. Wearing a small red backpack on the back. Cheerful, enthusiastic expression. Age appearance: 3-4 years old toddler proportions.

Character 3 — Grandpa Snail (Snail, elderly):
Front view and side view. A gentle snail with a brown spiral-patterned shell on his back and a soft light green body. Two long eye stalks on the head, wearing small gold-rimmed round glasses. A checkered scarf around his neck. Wise, kind, gentle expression. Slower and more thoughtful looking than the toddler characters.

All characters in children's picture book style, round soft shapes, simple lines, consistent design suitable for 0-5 year old readers.
