# Cover

Children's picture book cover illustration, soft watercolor style, warm bright spring colors, round shapes, simple composition, for 0-5 years old.

A chubby little butterfly (round body, large rainbow-gradient wings from pink to orange-yellow to light purple with white polka dots, big round black eyes, two curly antennae, wearing a small pale yellow sun hat with a daisy) and a round chubby little bee (yellow-and-black stripes, small transparent wings, big round eyes, rosy cheeks, small red backpack) fly together above a field of colorful spring flowers. The bee is cheerfully pulling the butterfly's little foot forward. The butterfly looks a bit shy, wings slightly folded.

Background: a bright spring garden bursting with colorful flowers — tulips, daisies, sunflowers — under a blue sky with fluffy white clouds.

Title text in playful rounded Chinese font at the top: "小蝴蝶的春日花园旅行"

Consistent character design, warm spring sunlight, joyful adventurous mood.
